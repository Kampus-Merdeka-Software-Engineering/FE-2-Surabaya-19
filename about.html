Our History

Nazarick Restaurant was founded in 2022 by two friends, John and Mary. John was a chef who had worked in a variety of restaurants, while Mary was a business graduate with a passion for food. The two friends decided to open a restaurant that would offer a variety of delicious and affordable food and beverages. They named the restaurant Nazarick, after the fictional city in the anime series Overlord. Nazarick Restaurant quickly became a popular destination for people in Surabaya, Indonesia. The restaurant is known for its high-quality ingredients, delicious food, and friendly service. In 2023, Nazarick Restaurant was awarded the "Best Restaurant in Surabaya" award. This award is a testament to the restaurant's commitment to providing its customers with a satisfying dining experience.
